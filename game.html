The Art Dealer Game

How to play: The goal is to guess the dealers pattern of buying
1. Select a game mode: K-2 is least difficult and 6-8 is most difficult
2. Choose 4 cards and see what the dealer buys
3. Your score is updated with how many cards the buyer bought
4. When the dealer buys 4 cards choose an option in the dropdown
5. Click check solution under the dropdown you selected
